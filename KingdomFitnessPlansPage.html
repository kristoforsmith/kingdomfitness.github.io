Workout Programs

Kingdom Fitness offers two different workout plans to help you meet your goals.

28 Day Challenge

A short term kickstarter program that will get you back on track and move you toward your fitness goals. You will lose fat, build muscle, and feel amazing as you follow Joseph's personalized programming for your exercise, nutrition, and accountability.

Gold Crown Program

In this program you will have an unbelievable amount of access to Joseph as your personal trainer. He will be programming every workout, giving you access to his customized meal plans, and staying in regular contact with you throughout the entire process. Those who commit to this program find that there is no need to spend large amounts of money on trainers at local gyms. Joseph works with each person individually and helps them attain their long term, challenging fitness goals.
