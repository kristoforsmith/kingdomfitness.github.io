What is Kingdom Fitness?

Kingdom Fitness consists of online fitness training geared toward those who want to lose fat, gain muscle, and transform their lives for the better. We offer two plans that can be customized to help you meet your health and fitness goals! If you would like more information about these plans, check out ourplans page.

Strategic Work Out Plan

Challenging work out plan that fits YOUR specific fitness and health goals, as well as your schedule!

Customizeable Meal Plan

Meal plan set up to meet your nurtrional and weight needs with customizeable options (even healthy snacks)!

Helpful Mobile App

Track your progress, connect with your trainer Joseph, watch tutorial videos, and log your meals!

Personal Trainer

Receive the fitness and health coaching YOU need through the help and encouragment of a professional trainer!

Meet Joseph.

Kingdom Fitness is owned and operated by health and fitness expert Joseph Chestnut who creates and maintains each client's program himself to assure success.
